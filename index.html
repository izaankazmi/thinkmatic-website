About Thinkmatic

A team that believes in lasting change

Thinkmatic is a multidisciplinary organization dedicated to helping children and adults reach their full potential. We are committed to making support accessible to families by providing personalized care in a comfortable and effective setting.

By working closely with our clients, we strive to create meaningful, lasting change — because together, we can make a difference.

All therapies available under one roof — no need to travel between multiple providers
Personalized care plans tailored to each child or adult's specific goals
Remediation available in both English and Urdu to serve Karachi's diverse families
🌟

Our mission

To make high-quality therapeutic support accessible to every family — providing a warm, effective space where children and adults can grow, heal, and thrive.

The people behind the care

Meet our team

A dedicated group of specialists committed to helping every child and adult reach their full potential.

Co-Founder

Samia Mujtaba

Co-Founder & Remedial Therapist

Co-founder of ThinkMatic with 30+ years in multi-sensory education, specialising in support for children with learning challenges. Credentialed from Hornsby Institute, London, and the Institute of Multi-Sensory Education, USA. Previously Director at Readyslexics (READ), Samia also serves on the boards of the Pakistan Centre for Autism and the Kiran Foundation.

Co-Founder

Mariya Moochhala

Co-Founder & Psychologist

Director of ThinkMatic and clinical psychologist with over 25 years of experience. Specialises in psychological assessments for ADHD, Learning Disabilities, and Neurodevelopmental disorders, working with children from 2½ years to adults. Offers counselling with a particular focus on those with disabilities.

Insia Kausari

Remedial Therapist

Holds a diploma from READ and is a certified Registered Behaviour Technician (RBT). Extensive experience supporting students with learning difficulties in reading, spelling, composition, and comprehension, using evidence-based strategies and behaviour management techniques.

Mohsina Khan

Remedial Therapist

Certified Montessori teacher from London School of Montessori and Remedial Therapist certified by CARE in Therapeutic Techniques and Supervised Language Training. Specialises in helping children overcome learning disabilities in reading, writing, and spelling.

Sobia Ali

Remedial Therapist & Counsellor

Holds a READ-Hornsby Diploma alongside a background in Clinical Psychology. Works as both a Remedial Therapist and counsellor, supporting students with learning differences in English, Urdu, and maths. Uses technological tools to create engaging, multi-sensory learning environments.

Ayesha Khan

Psychologist

Humanistic and psychodynamic integrative therapist with a Certificate in Humanistic Counselling and a Diploma in Integrative Therapy (both CPCAB, UK). Over 20 years in the education sector. Passionate about connecting people with the support they need and breaking the stigma around mental health.

Fatima Soomro

Speech Therapist

Speech-language therapist with a Bachelor's from Ziauddin University, specialising in Autism Spectrum Disorder and language disorders. Dedicated to helping children and adults with communication, language development, and social interaction challenges using evidence-based interventions.

Farah Khan

Speech Therapist

Specialised Speech and Language Pathologist and Audiologist with a focus on paediatric speech and hearing. Expert in early intervention, AAC, IEPs, Tympanometry, Play Audiometry, and cochlear implant programming. Dedicated to empowering children to thrive in their communication skills.

Alliya Haidery

Behaviour Therapist (BCBA)

Board Certified Behaviour Analyst with 17 years of experience in Integrated Therapies, Special Needs, Autism, and Inclusion. Holds an MSc in Physiotherapy and MA in Special Education. Currently CEO of Pakistan Centre for Autism (PCA) and an internationally approved CEU provider.

Kanwal Fatima

Behaviour Therapist

Paediatric Psychologist and certified Behaviour Therapist with a Master's in Psychology (Rehabilitation). 8+ years of experience in psycho-diagnostic and psychoeducational assessments for children aged 2–12. International experience in the UAE working across diverse cultural backgrounds.

Shumaila Talib

Manager of Operations

The organisational backbone of ThinkMatic since 2018, Shumaila manages appointments, coordinates schedules, and oversees daily operations with professionalism and warmth. Her dedication ensures every client and therapist is looked after from the moment they walk in.

Saleem

Office Watchman & Guardian Angel
